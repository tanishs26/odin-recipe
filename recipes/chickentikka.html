Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is a popular Indian dish made with marinated and grilled chicken chunks served in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce. It is typically enjoyed with rice or naan bread.

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, cayenne pepper, and salt.
  2. Add chicken cubes to the marinade, cover, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  3. Preheat the grill or broiler. Thread marinated chicken onto skewers and grill until cooked through and slightly charred.
  4. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped onions, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Saut√© until onions are soft and translucent.
  5. Add garam masala and paprika, cook for a minute.
  6. Stir in tomato puree and cook for 5-7 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  7. Add grilled chicken to the sauce, stir to coat well.
  8. Pour in heavy cream, mix, and simmer for a few minutes until heated through.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan bread.
